HieroLingo is an educational app for learning Egyptian hieroglyphs.

It contains:
- Guided learning chapters (alphabet, vocabulary, royal names, cartouches, verbs)
- Interactive quizzes (multiple choice, matching, puzzles)
- Compendium (dictionary of glyphs, Gardiner list, translations)
- Streak system, coins, hearts
- Profile customization and themes
- Cloud sync (Firebase)

The user navigates between lessons, quizzes, compendium, shop, and progress tracking.

Goal of the agent:
Explore the app structure and understand all main sections.
